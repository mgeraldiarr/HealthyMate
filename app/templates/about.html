Definition

What is HealthyMate?

HealthyMate is a smart web-based application designed as your daily personal nutrition companion. We combine calorie need calculation technology with direct consultation access to health experts to democratize healthy living for everyone.

We realize that every individual has different physical conditions, activity levels, and body metabolisms. Therefore, HealthyMate rejects a one-size-fits-all nutrition approach and focuses on a personalized method tailored specifically for you.

Background

Why HealthyMate was Created?

Faced with the complexities of modern health issues and nutrition misinformation, we are committed to delivering simple, reliable, and science-based solutions.

Modern Health Challenges

The rising consumption of high-calorie instant processed foods combined with a sedentary lifestyle triggers a spike in obesity rates and various metabolic diseases in society.

Nutrition Misinformation

The abundance of extreme diet guides on the internet often confuses the public. Without personalized metabolic calculations, such diet patterns risk harming the body's health.

Medical Access Barriers

Direct consultation with nutritionists or medical specialists often requires high costs and long queue times, preventing people from getting formal health advice.

Service Pillars

Core Features of HealthyMate

Providing a comprehensive health support ecosystem ranging from nutrition calculators to direct medical consultations.

Smart Calorie Calculator

Helps calculate total daily energy expenditure (TDEE) and BMR accurately based on your body metrics and daily physical activity level.

Nutritional Meal Recommendations

Formulates a daily meal plan with balanced macronutrient details (carbs, protein, fat) tailored to your weight goals.

Direct Doctor Consultation

Allows you to book and consult privately in a chat room with certified doctors regarding your nutritional health concerns.

Progress Tracking

Monitors your weight, height, and nutrition history periodically through easy-to-understand visual statistics on the dashboard.

Vision & Mission

Vision & Mission of HealthyMate

We move forward with a long-term commitment to create a nutrition-conscious society that has the independence to manage their physical health preventatively.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted digital health companion platform in Indonesia that democratizes personalized nutrition management and connects medical care inclusively.

Our Mission

1

To provide smart nutrition calculators that are accurate, user-friendly, and adjusted to the user's actual physical activity.

2

To present science-based, balanced nutritional meal recommendations that are easy to practice daily.

3

To open gates of instant and secure communication between patients and certified healthcare practitioners for valid medical nutrition guidance.
