Our Story

Our Story

HealthyMate was developed as part of our final semester project to fulfill the requirements for the 4th Semester Final Exam at Esa Unggul University, Bekasi. Stemming from the need for an informative, practical, and accessible nutrition tool, we created a technology-based solution to help people understand their daily nutritional needs.

Inspired by our coursework and personal experiences in maintaining a healthy lifestyle, we designed a web application that provides a calorie calculator and data-based healthy eating recommendations. What began as a class assignment has evolved into a functional application that we're proud to share for broader benefit.

Our Development Journey

2025

Project Conception

Our team formed and brainstormed ideas for our final project. We chose to focus on nutrition technology after recognizing the lack of Indonesia-focused solutions.

2025

Research Phase

We conducted in-depth research through literature studies on nutritional science, dietary guidelines, and existing similar solutions as references for application development.

2025

Development Begins

Started building the website with core features using Python Flask for backend and Tailwind CSS for frontend. Implemented the calorie calculator first.

2025

Final Submission

Completed all features, conducted testing, and prepared project submission. Presented HealthyMate to our lecturers and classmates.
